ROCKET LEAGUE

What is Rocket league?

Rocket league is a car soccer game, that conists of 1 to 4 players in teams of two that plaay against each other in field with a ball.
The objective of the game is to use your car to score goals in under 5 minutes.
If the time limit is over and one player has a higher goal difference than the opponent,
then the player wins. if both teams have the same number of goals then the match will be taken to over time and the winner will be the first one to score.
There are different types of game modes in the game but all of them return to the same objective.
there are differnt types of playlists and training packs,
where you can either play alone or with other plyers that range from 2-4 in one team.


Why I love the game!

the reason i love this game is because its a fast-paced game where you are able to show your skills and mechanics in the field as you play with your team mates or alone.
it also is really fun to play with your friends, and you can be creative with your mechanics and show how good of a player you are.
the game requires a lot of game time and practice to be able to use mechanics properly as it gets more competitive as you progress in the game.
