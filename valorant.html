VALORANT

game explanation

Valorant is a fist-person tactical shooter where two teams of 5 fight for an objective.
there will be two teams, one team will be defending sites in a map, and the other team
will be holding a bomb that needs to be planted in a site and defend it until it explodes or all the opponents are eleminated.
each teams will be selecting different characters and each character will have different abilities to help achieve their objective.
in order to win there should be coordination and team communication to achieve these objectives.
there are different game modes in the game each of them have different number of rounds.
